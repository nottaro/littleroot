- Matrices are a way of graphically storing certain data.

$$\begin{bmatrix}  
x\\  
y  
\end{bmatrix}$$


## Matrix Addition

$$\begin{bmatrix}  
a & b\\  
c & d   
\end{bmatrix} +

\begin{bmatrix}  
w & x\\  
y & z  
\end{bmatrix} = 

\begin{bmatrix}  
w+a & x+b\\  
y+c & z+d  
\end{bmatrix}$$ 
- 2 matrices can be added if and only if their dimensions are the same
