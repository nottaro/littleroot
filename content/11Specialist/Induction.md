## "For all" statements
- Sometimes in proofs, we get "for all statements"
- An example would be, "for all integers n,  n to the power of 3, take n, is divisible by 3"
- This would be written as: $n^3 - n = 3k, k \in \mathbb{Z^+}, \forall \ n \in \mathbb{Z^+}$
