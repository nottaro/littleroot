- Element: 
- Molecule
	- Ozone ($O_3$)
	- $HCl_{(g)}$
- Compound: Substance made of atoms of different elements bonded together chemically
	- $NaCl$
	- $HCl_{(g)}$
- Mixture
	- Homogenous
		- $NaOH_{(aq)}$
		- $HCl_{(aq)}$
	- Heterogenous
		- Milk, concrete
- Pure Substances: 
	- Ozone ($O_3$)
	- $HCl_{(g)}$
	- $Ne$
